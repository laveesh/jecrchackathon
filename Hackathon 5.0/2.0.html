ABOUT JECRC HACKATHON 5.0


Hackathon a computer oriented event which includes computer programing, software development, graphic designing, interface designing, app development, website designing, video game development, operating system development and every single thing related to coding. A hackathon is usually a day long team oriented (can be 24 hours, 36 hours, etc.) coding competition where software programmers, developers, designers, etc. come together to build and design something cool. Hackathon is organized by various institutions , companies, colleges and organisation all have their own purpose for conducting it. Winner team is awarded.

The Jecrc Hackathon 5.0 brings a digital product development platform and takes you on a new voyage of the hack with talented teams of developers, designers, and data scientists that blend innovation with creativity and construct solutions out of it, making your experience peerless and one of a kind. Team up with us on this unique and exciting journey in Jaipur on 25th March. 2022 to 26th March 2022.

Frequently asked questions

What is Hackathon?

Hacking is creative problem solving.(It does not have to involve technology.)
A hackathon is any event of any duration where people come together to solve interesting business problems and real world challenges. Most of the hackathons have a parallel track of workshops.
Participants typically form groups of about 2-6 individuals, take out their laptops(if the event is technology themed), and dive into problems. Training workshops are a great parallel track especially for newcomers but also for all participants. The main purpose of hackathon is:
check_boxGetting students intersted in coding.
 check_boxTurning ideas into reality.
 check_boxMaking student come in team,coordinate with each other and work in competitive environment.

How do we Submit our idea?

PLEASE NOTE: Idea submission must be nade by team leader of a team only
One Team can submit ONLY ONE Idea
Submission dates should be strictly followed. No exceptions will be made.
Participants should submit their innovation by filling form available on website.
After filling the form which is in pdf format they have to submit it to the college with Rs.600.
Entries to be sent only in the prescribed format; otherwise they are bound to get rejected.
Requests for the changes in the Idea will not be entertained after the last date of registration. Teams cannot edit any team details after the submission of form.

What is Judgment criteria?

Judgement will be done by the developer experts who are working in MNC's and are having the experience of 4-5 years in their field.
In the event of 24 hours, the judgement will be done at every 12 hours where the participants have to show their work what they have done so far.
Team members are free to ask regarding suggestion and improvement in their project by our judges once the judgement round gets over.

What is selection criteria?

Evaluation criteria will include novelty of the idea,complexity,clarity and details in the prescribed format, feasibility, practicability, sustainability, scale of impact,user experience and potential for future work progression
The judging of the hackathon is generally based on the demo presentation given by the team.
The 3 main judge decides the final winner according to the different criteria such as:-


    Visibility
    Awesomeness
    Technological Stack
    Impact Created
